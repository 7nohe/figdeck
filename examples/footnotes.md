## Summary

Footnotes are useful for:

1. Citations and references[^cite]
2. Additional context[^context]
3. Technical notes[^tech]

[^cite]: Academic papers, books, and online sources
[^context]: Without cluttering the main content
[^tech]: Implementation details or caveats
